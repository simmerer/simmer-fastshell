Hi. I'm David.

My two decades of deep engineering, design, and leadership expertise has been indispensable to a variety of startups, established businesses, educational institutions, and non-profits around the world.

I'm currently full-time at Rinse SF, helping to lead a small but stellar team building the best clothing care service in the country.

Prior to moving to the Bay Area, I ran a successful solo agency in downtown Minneapolis, and was the CTO & technical co-founder at Medgineering, where we were ahead of the game in building one of the first online marketplaces for medical device quality & regulatory consultants. Medgineering was acquired in 2017.

Throughout my career, I've produced a huge variety of online application development, branding, design, and product guidance.

When you're in need of signature creative & technical work, let's talk.
